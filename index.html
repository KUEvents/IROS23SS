Special Session: Artificial Intelligence for Autonomous Vehicle Perception

01-05 October, Detroit, Michigan, USA

About Special Session

About AI4AVP Special Session

Artificial Intelligence for Autonomous Vehicle Perception (AI4AVP) is a proposed special session in the 2023 IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems (IROS 2023).

Overview

As we are moving into an increasingly AI-driven world, it is clear that the future of autonomous vehicles (AVs) will be deeply intertwined with artificial intelligence. While AVs are not yet the norm, they are already here and will soon transform the norms of passenger transit, freight, robotaxi, shuttle service, home delivery, logistics, and so on. From self-driving features to in-vehicle infotainment, AI will play a vital role in making our driving experience safer, and more efficient. However, in autonomous systems, the high complexity of environments makes vehicle perception one of the most challenging tasks. For instance, collision-free trajectory forecasting, collaborative decision-making, teleoperation, and operating in adverse weather conditions can be regarded as some of the critical features of real-world autonomous mobility applications. Using multi-task learning, these critical autonomous vehicle perception tasks can be integrated into a unified framework.

The goal of this special session is for the groups of researchers at the intersection of AI and AVs to have a dedicated session to meet and facilitate a collaborative forum for discussion of current efforts in AI and autonomous driving systems, especially conducting interdisciplinary research in this community. The overarching themes of the contributing papers are the technological advances that improve vehicle perception with AI for smarter and safer autonomous vehicles of the future.

Topical Keywords

Through this special session, we will focus on technical research being done in the field of autonomous vehicles and AI integration. Topics of interest include, but are not limited to:

  • AI for autonomous vehicles (car, ship, UAV, USV, etc.)
  • Collaborative autonomous driving
  • Collision-free route prediction for AVs
  • Underwater autonomous navigation systems
  • Visual perception in structured/unstructured environments
  • Teleoperator assistive autonomous systems
  • Multi-agent systems for AV
  • Collection, curation, and sampling of AV datasets
  • Behavior prediction of pedestrians, passengers, or other dynamic agents
  • AI solutions for enhancing the passenger experience
  • Privacy and cybersecurity features for AV
  • Trust in AV
  • Ethical issues for AI-enabled autonomous driving

Submission

For accepted special sessions, papers organized under that session must be submitted via PaperPlaza. As is the case for regular papers, the submission deadline for AI4AV special session papers is March 1, 2023. Papers should be six pages (US letter size) in length, with up to two extra pages (subject to extra page charges after the acceptance). Papers will undergo the same peer review and decision process as all other regular papers and, if accepted, be published in the proceedings.

After submitting your paper to AI4AVP special session, please email your submission ID, author list, and manuscript title to the principal organizers by March 5, 2023: Bilal Hassan , Jorge Dias , Majid Khonji .

The submission details and template are the same as the IROS 2022 submission process, and can be found Here.
