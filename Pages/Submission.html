Submission Details

Papers for AI4AMR special session must be submitted via Paper Plaza. The submission deadline for this special session is March 1, 2023. Papers will undergo the same peer review and decision process as all other regular papers and, if accepted, will be published in the IROS 2023 proceedings.

Author Guidelines

Papers should be six pages (US letter size) in length, with up to two extra pages (subject to extra page charges after the acceptance). All papers must be in English and submitted electronically in PDF (up to 6 MB) format. The below-mentioned guidelines and templates should be followed while drafting the manuscript to ensure formatting compliance (e.g., margins, fonts, and size):

  • LaTex Template
  • MS Word Template

    • In addition, a 1-minute video (up to 10 MB) can be added to supplement your paper. Videos are not mandatory; rather, it’s an opportunity to augment one’s paper to visibly showcase results. MPG, MPEG, or MP4 video formats can be used. One can upload or re-upload the video attachment no later than March 7, 2023 (23:59 PST). More details about the submission process can be referred to from the ( IROS 2023 Webpage).

    After submitting your paper to AI4AMR special session, please email your submission ID, author list, and manuscript title to the principal organizers by March 5, 2023: Bilal Hassan (bilal.hassan@ku.ac.ae), Jorge Dias (jorge.dias@ku.ac.ae), Majid Khonji (majid.khoni@ku.ac.ae).
